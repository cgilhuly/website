Colleen Gilhuly

PhD candidate, Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics, University of Toronto

I am a PhD student at the University of Toronto working with Prof. Roberto Abraham and Allison Merritt (MPIA). Together with Prof. Pieter van Dokkum's group at Yale University and Prof. Charlie Conroy at Harvard University, our group operates the Dragonfly Telephoto Array. I am leading the Dragonfly Edge-on Galaxies Survey in order to explore the faint outer regions of spiral galaxies that are not easily seen by conventional telescopes. Focusing on edge-on galaxies minimizes the amount of area on the sky where the bright disc component dominates over faint features of interest, particularly the stellar halo.
